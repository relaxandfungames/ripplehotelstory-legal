Privacy Policy for Ripple Hotel Story

Effective Date: May 04, 2026

Last Updated: May 04, 2026

1. Introduction

Relax and Fun Games ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use the "Ripple Hotel Story" mobile simulation game (the "App").

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information Stored On Your Device

The App stores information locally on your device to provide game features and save your progress, including but not limited to:

  • Game progress and unlocks
  • Player preferences (such as chosen language, gender/character, sound and music settings)

This information is stored only on your device and is NOT transmitted to our servers.

2.2 Information Collected by Third-Party Services

Some features of the App rely on third-party services which may collect data for their own purposes:

Google Play Services

  • Used for App distribution, app updates, billing, and crash reporting
  • Collected data: See Google's Privacy Policy

AdMob (Google AdSense for Mobile Apps)

  • The App displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob at natural breaks in play
  • AdMob and its partners may collect device identifiers (such as the Advertising ID) and non-personal usage data to serve and personalize ads
  • For more information, visit How Google uses data
  • You may opt out of personalized ads in your device privacy settings

3. How We Use Information

Locally stored information is used to:

  • Save and restore your game progress
  • Remember your language and gameplay preferences
  • Provide a personalized and enjoyable gaming experience

No game progress, choices, or preferences are collected by us, transmitted to us, or sold by us.

4. Data Storage and Security

All personal data related to your progress and preferences is stored only on your device using secure Android mechanisms. We do not operate any server for this App and do not collect, process, or store your personal data.

5. Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. If you believe your child has provided personal data in the App without your consent, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have full control over your data stored in the App:

  • Delete your data and progress by uninstalling the App
  • Reset progress and clear preferences within the App (if the reset option is available)
  • Opt out of personalized advertising in your Android device's privacy/ad settings

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as our App and practices evolve. If we make significant changes, we will update the date above and provide notice in the App when possible.
